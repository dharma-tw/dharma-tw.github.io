Heart Sutra in Sanskrit (Full Text)

 

The recitation of Heart Sutra (full text) is principally based on an original Text of "Chinese transliteration" derived from a Saint, Hsuan Chuang (602-664), a Honorable Buddhist monk/master in the Tang Dynasty in China.

The contents of this presentation are inclusive of an Introduction, Reference Datum and Notes described in English regarding the background.

Five (5) editing copies of Heart Sutra in the different languages are also enclosed in this presentation for reference. 

For detail about the background of this presentation, please refer to the Heart Sutra website in Chinese:
